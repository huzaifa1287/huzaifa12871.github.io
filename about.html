Locations

New York City

Squarespace’s 150,000-square-foot headquarters are located in Manhattan’s West Village at 8 Clarkson Street. The office fills five floors, in addition to a dedicated lobby and roof deck, within the historic Maltz Building. The space was designed with a focus on creating collaborative working spaces for employees to solve problems together creatively.

Learn More
Portland

Squarespace Portland opened in 2014 and is located in the Spalding Building on SW Washington Street in the downtown area of the city. Just around the corner are gourmet food trucks, green spaces, and an array of cultural offerings. The 25,000-square-foot office houses one hub of our Customer Operations team.

Learn More
Dublin

The Squarespace Dublin office was founded in 2013 as a hub for our Customer Operations team. The 24,589-square-foot office space is located at Le Pole House in Dublin 8, close to Antique Quarter, The Liberties, and original city walls. Squarespace occupies four floors with a dedicated entrance, lobby, and roof terrace.
